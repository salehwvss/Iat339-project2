HOW CAN I TRACK MY ORDERS and PAYMENT?

After logging into your account, the status of your checkout history can be found under Order History.

HOW DO I CANCEL MY ORDERS BEFORE I MAKE A PAYMENT?

After logging into your account, go to your Shopping Cart. Here, you will be able to make payment or cancel your order

WHAT ARE THE PAYMENT METHODS AVAILABLE?

At the moment, we only accept Credit/Debit cards and Paypal payments

HOW CAN I CHANGE MY SHIPPING ADDRESS?

By default, the last used shipping address will be saved into your Sample Store account. When you are checking out your order, the default shipping address will be displayed and you have the option to amend it if you need to.

HOW LONG WILL IT TAKE FOR MY ORDER TO ARRIVE AFTER I MAKE PAYMENT?

It usually takes between 10 to 14 business days

HOW DO YOU SHIP MY ORDERS?

We will ship your order via CanadaPost Air Mail
